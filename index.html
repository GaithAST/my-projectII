The Datset¶

The dataset I will be analysing for this project is the TMDB Movie Dataset. It has the following columns:

'id',

'imdb_id',

'popularity',

'budget': budget of movie before inflation,

'revenue': revenue of movie before inflation,

'original_title': original title of the movie,

'cast': list of the main characters' cast seperated with |,

'homepage': link to the website of the movie,

'director': the name of the director of the movie,

'tagline': tagline of the movie,

'keywords': keywords related to the movie,

'overview': a breif descreprion about the events of the movie,

'runtime': the length of the movie in minutes,

'genres': genres related to the movie,

'production_companies': production companies involved in making the movie,

'release_date': release date of the movie,

vote_count': amount of people who voted their rating for the movie,

'vote_average': average of votes,

'release_year': which year was the movie released,

'budget_adj': budget of the movie, but changed to inflation,

'revenue_adj': budget of the movie, but changed to inflation
